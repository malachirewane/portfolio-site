About Me

Hello! I’m Malachi Rewane, a Networking and Information Technology Security student passionate about technology, programming, and creative projects. I enjoy learning how different systems connect and work together, whether it’s setting up secure networks, exploring cybersecurity concepts, or building websites that combine both functionality and design.

Outside of school, I like tackling challenges that sharpen my skills, such as Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions, exploring new software tools, and working on hands-on projects that push me to think creatively. I also have experience in IT support, where I learned how valuable adaptability and problem-solving are when helping people with technical issues.

I’m driven by curiosity, creativity, and a strong interest in how technology can make everyday life more efficient and secure. My goal is to continue growing into a versatile IT professional with a focus on networking and cybersecurity.
